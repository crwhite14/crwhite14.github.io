About

I design algorithms that create advanced AI algorithms.

I am Head of Research at Abacus.AI in San Francisco. Our work focuses on AutoML, explainability, bias, and other areas of machine learning. I am particularly interested in neural architecture search, hyperparameter optimization, and explaining predictions made by ML models.

I graduated from Carnegie Mellon University with a Ph.D. in computer science. I was fortunate to be advised by Nina Balcan, and I was supported by the NDSEG Fellowship. I received my undergraduate degree from Amherst College.

For more, see my CV (last updated Apr. 2022).
