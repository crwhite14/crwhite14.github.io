My CV (Updated Jan. 2019).




I design algorithms which create advanced AI algorithms.

I am a Research Scientist at RealityEngines.AI in San Francisco. I recently graduated from Carnegie Mellon University with a Ph.D. in computer science. My research is in machine learning and theoretical computer science. I am particularly interested in autoML, specifically, neural architecture search and hyperparameter optimization.

During my Ph.D., I was supported by the National Defense Science and Engineering Graduate (NDSEG) Fellowship, and I was fortunate to be advised by Nina Balcan. I received my undergraduate degree from Amherst College.
