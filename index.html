About

I design algorithms that create advanced AI algorithms.

I am a Research Scientist at Abacus.AI (previously called RealityEngines.AI) in San Francisco. Before that, I graduated from Carnegie Mellon University with a Ph.D. in computer science. My research spans machine learning and theoretical computer science. I am particularly interested in AutoML, including neural architecture search, hyperparameter optimization, and automated data augmentation.

During my Ph.D., I was fortunate to be advised by Nina Balcan, and I was supported by the NDSEG Fellowship. I received my undergraduate degree from Amherst College.

For more, see my CV (updated Jan. 2019).
